## What building in public really means

It’s not just posting updates.

It’s sharing:

- your process
- your wins
- your failures

Honestly.

## Why it works

People follow journeys, not products.

When you share consistently:

- people trust you
- people care about your progress
- people become users

## What to share

Keep it simple:

- what you’re building today
- what you learned
- what didn’t work

No need for perfection.

## Consistency matters more than quality

Posting once a week won’t work.

You need frequency.

Even small updates matter.

## Don’t try to go viral

Focus on:

- clarity
- honesty
- relevance

Not virality.

## Engage with people

Reply to comments.

Talk to others.

Be part of the conversation.

## The long-term effect

Over time, you build:

- audience
- trust
- opportunities

That’s the real value.

## Final thoughts

Build in public is simple:

Share. Learn. Repeat.

That’s it.
